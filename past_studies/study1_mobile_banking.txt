STUDY 1 - Mobile banking app usability (8 interviews)

"I tried to pay a friend but the steps weren't in any obvious order, so I gave
up and used a different app."
"The instructions for setting up a new payee were buried behind a tiny link."
"Text on the confirmation screen is too small, I can never tell if it worked."
"I worry about security but there's no clear sign my money actually moved."
"Logging in takes too many taps and I do it ten times a day."
